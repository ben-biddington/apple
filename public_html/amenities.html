Things to Do in the Area

Five minutes drive along the coast to Marahau brings you to the start of the Abel Tasman National Park where a water taxi can take you to almost anywhere you would like to go within the park. Further activities within the area include; walking, kayaking, rafting, swimming, snorkeling, sailing, diving, fishing, skiing/wakeboarding, charter boat/helicopter tours, sky-diving, rock climbing, winery’s, arts and crafts, fine dining or just relaxing on one of our beaches. (NB some tourism operators are based in Motueka or Kaiteriteri, but pick up can be arranged.)
